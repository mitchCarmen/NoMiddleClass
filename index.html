Key Takeaways

1

The Middle Class Was Built, Then Dismantled

Policy choices‚Äîunions, taxes, homeownership programs‚Äîcreated the middle class. Different policy choices reversed it.

2

CEO Pay Decoupled from Worker Pay

CEOs gained 1,094% while workers gained 26%. Stock-based pay created a separate wealth track.

3

We're Back to 1929 Levels

Wealth concentration has returned to pre-Depression peaks. The top 0.1% now holds 22% of wealth.

4

Other Models Exist

Nordic countries achieve similar GDP with far less inequality. The US in the 1950s-70s achieved it too.
